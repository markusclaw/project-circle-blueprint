⭕ Project Circle

The Anti-Dating App: AI-Powered Connection Through Self-Knowledge

Vision & Philosophy

Project Circle is a connection platform that inverts the dating app paradigm. Instead of infinite swiping, algorithmic theater, and monetized loneliness, Project Circle uses AI to help users discover themselves and then find meaningful connections within their natural circles.

Core Thesis

Modern dating apps are engagement machines, not matching systems. They profit from keeping users single, swiping endlessly through curated fiction. This creates:

  • Fatigue and cynicism around connection
  • Performative profiles that favor the most "developed" (often fake)
  • No reward for authenticity
  • Endless choice paralysis instead of meaningful matching

Project Circle fixes this by treating the app as a tool for self-knowledge first, connection second. We help users understand themselves, then connect them with people who actually align—using degrees of separation as a map, not random cards.

The Name: Circles

In real life, we date, marry, befriend, and do business within our natural circles (3-4 degrees of separation from us). Project Circle formalizes this insight: instead of infinite undifferentiated swiping, the app builds your circles of compatible people, making degrees of separation visible and intentional.

Jungian Foundation

Project Circle is built on Carl Jung's personality framework (via MBTI). We treat personality types not as fixed boxes, but as a language for self-understanding and compatibility. Users evolve; their profiles evolve. The app learns them through interactions, not just a static bio.

The Problem: Why Dating Apps Suck

Current Landscape Issues

  • Monopoly economics: Most apps owned by same parent company (Match Group), same philosophy: keep people single, extract maximum revenue
  • Swiping is theater: Profiles are curated fiction. Only over-developed, "scammy" profiles succeed
  • No authenticity reward: Real people get buried. Performative people thrive
  • Engagement over matching: Design optimized for time-in-app and premium conversions, not actual matches
  • Fatigue and cynicism: Users hate the process. They know it's broken
Validation: User interviews confirm this. Most people hate dating apps despite using them. They see through the design, recognize the monetization trap, and feel exhausted by the swiping treadmill.

The Solution: Project Circle

Core Features (By Principle)

1. Evolving Profiles Through Conversation

Instead of a frozen bio + photos, profiles build over time through interaction. AI learns the user through:

  • Onboarding session (basic info, initial personality assessment)
  • Guided conversation flows (AI asks questions, learns their values, style, goals)
  • Interaction analysis (how they talk, what matters to them, their patterns)
  • Continuous refinement (profile updates as user reveals more)

2. AI-Powered Matching (Not Swiping)

No endless cards. Instead:

  • AI analyzes personality (MBTI framework), values, lifestyle, goals
  • System calculates compatibility (not just type match, but deeper alignment)
  • Presents meaningful connections with reasoning ("You both value authenticity + adventure")
  • Reduces fatigue: quality over quantity

3. Degrees of Separation (Circles)

Makes social proximity visible:

  • Degree 1: Direct connections (people you match with)
  • Degree 2: Friends of friends (people in your circles' networks)
  • Degree 3-4: Extended circles (growing proximity-aware recommendations)
  • Users see why someone is suggested (degree + compatibility reason)

4. Self-Knowledge as Primary Output

Helping users understand themselves is as valuable as finding matches:

  • Personality insights delivered over time
  • Values clarification through interaction
  • Compatibility patterns users can learn from
  • Growth-oriented feedback (not judgment)

5. Minimal, Introspective Design

Visual language should feel:

  • Calm: Opposite of frantic swiping
  • Intentional: Every interaction meaningful
  • Introspective: Like uncovering, not performing
  • Premium: Sophisticated, not cluttered
  • Human-centric: Warm, not mechanical

Data Model: The Personality Engine

Core Dimensions

Personality Profile (Jungian/MBTI Framework):

  • Extraversion ↔ Introversion (E/I)
  • Sensing ↔ Intuition (S/N)
  • Thinking ↔ Feeling (T/F)
  • Judging ↔ Perceiving (J/P)
  • Result: 16 types as a lens, not a cage

Extended Dimensions

  • Values: What matters (family, adventure, creativity, stability, etc.)
  • Lifestyle: How they live (urban/rural, active/reflective, social/solitary)
  • Life Stage: Current phase (career-building, settling down, exploring, etc.)
  • Interests & Goals: What they do, where they're headed
  • Communication Style: How they talk, connect, process conflict

Interaction Tracking

AI learns through:

  • Conversation patterns (tone, depth, reciprocity)
  • Response authenticity (consistency over time)
  • Connection outcomes (mutual interest, conversation quality)
  • Feedback loops (user confirms/corrects profile insights)

Compatibility Algorithm (High-Level)

Compatibility = Type Resonance + Values Alignment + Lifestyle Fit + Growth Potential

  • Type Resonance: Complementary types, not just matches (e.g., INTJ × ENFP can work)
  • Values Alignment: Core values must overlap (even if interests differ)
  • Lifestyle Fit: Life pace, location, social needs align
  • Growth Potential: Can they grow together?

Technical Architecture

Stack (Phased Approach)

  • Frontend: React/Next.js (web) + React Native (mobile) — minimal, intentional UI
  • Backend: Node.js/Express or Python (personality engine + matching logic)
  • Database: PostgreSQL (user profiles, interaction history, compatibility scores)
  • AI/LLM: OpenAI/Claude API (conversation flows, profile generation, matching reasoning)
  • Real-time: WebSocket (live chat, notifications)
  • Infrastructure: Cloud (AWS/GCP/Cloudflare) — scalable, low-latency

Core Services

  • Auth Service: Account creation, identity verification
  • Personality Engine: MBTI assessment, profile building, interaction learning
  • Matching Engine: Compatibility scoring, circle detection, recommendation ranking
  • Conversation Service: AI-guided interactions, profile refinement
  • Messaging Service: User-to-user communication, moderation
  • Analytics Service: User behavior, matching quality, feedback loops

API-First Architecture

Clean separation: Frontend consumes well-defined APIs. Allows web + mobile + future platforms without rebuilding business logic.

Design Language

Principles

  • Minimal: Only what's necessary. Remove noise.
  • Introspective: Feels like self-discovery, not performance
  • Intentional: Every interaction is meaningful
  • Calm: Opposite of frantic/addictive design
  • Premium: Sophisticated, not cluttered
  • Human-centered: Warm, not mechanical

Visual Direction

  • Color: Muted neutrals + one accent color (calm, not loud)
  • Typography: Clean, readable sans-serif with generous whitespace
  • Interaction: Smooth, unhurried transitions
  • Metaphor: Uncovering layers, not swiping cards

Key Interactions

  • Onboarding: Guided conversation, not a form dump
  • Profile: Living document, evolves over time
  • Matching: Curated suggestions with reasoning ("Here's why we think you'd connect")
  • Messaging: Natural, focused conversations
  • Insights: Personality growth & compatibility patterns delivered thoughtfully

  • Mindset: Tired of traditional dating apps, seeking authentic connections
  • Values: Self-knowledge, authenticity, meaningful connection

Geographic Launch

Phase 1a: San Diego / Southern California region (founder's home market, tightly knit, good for early testing)

Phase 1b: Expand regionally (California, Southwest)

Phase 2: National / Global (as community grows)

Secondary Audiences (Later Phases)

  • Anyone seeking meaningful professional/business networks
  • People building communities around shared values
  • Users interested in personal growth + connection
